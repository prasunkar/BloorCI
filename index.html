Bloor CI

Bloor Collegiate Institute is a high school located at the intersection of Bloor St. and Dufferin St. It is home to ~850 brilliant students, working hard every day to make themselves and Bloor Collegiate Institute a name in the academic world.

Bloor CI is home to the TOPS (Talented Offerings in the Programmes of the Sciences) program. To learn more about it, click here.
